Project Overview

We chose the She Angels Foundation website because it’s mission is purposeful and meaningful which they give grants, mentorship support and resources to non-profit organization that is founded by women or girls. We hope through a user research and redesign that would allows She Angels Foundation be able to provide a more trustworthy pleasant user experience. She Angels was designed to provide funding for non-profit organizations founded by women. We have observed that the She Angels Foundation is not presenting itself as a trustworthy organization online, which is causing potential donors to patronize a different organization, resulting in less available grants for grant applicants.
